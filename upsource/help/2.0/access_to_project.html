Providing access to a project

You can give access rights to groups or specific users.

To give access to a group:

  1. Click the settings icon on the top right of the screen.

  2. The Administration view will open.

  3. Click Users. The Users view will be displayed.

  4. Click Groups at the top. The Groups view form will be displayed.

  5. Select the group from the list and click the Access tab.

  6. Click Grant Access then select Role or Permission and a project from a displayed menu.

To give access to a user:

  1. Click the settings icon on the top right of the screen.

  2. The Administration view will open.

  3. Click Users. The Users view will be displayed.

  4. Select the user from the list and click the Access tab.

  5. Click Grant Access then select Role or Permission and a project from the displayed menu.
