Issue Tracking Tools Survey 2016

We conducted a survey with over 1,500 participants to find out the most popular issue tracking tools and gain insight into how they are used by different members of software development teams.
Here are the results.

Disclaimer:

To promote this survey, we primarily used our own marketing channels. Naturally, we attracted a large number of JetBrains customers and audiences related to JetBrains in one way or another.

Cloud or server?

Why?

Company policy is a primary reason to choose a stand-alone version

Most participants (70-80%) use a stand-alone server when they choose a tool that also offers an incloud version.

Why?

  • cheaper
  • easier to maintain
  • more control
  • don’t trust clouds
  • company policy
