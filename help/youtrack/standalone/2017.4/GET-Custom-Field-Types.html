GET Custom Field Types

GET /admin/customfield/type

Get all available custom field types.

GET /rest/admin/customfield/type

For each available custom field type, its name and url - its endpoint - are returned.

Sample

Request:

GET /rest/admin/customfield/type HTTP/1.1 Host: https://youtrack.mycompany.com Authorization: Bearer perm:YWRtaW4=.UG9zdG1hbg==.oVCSLKlWh4WkBBLvLryMScl4iIiWwt Content-Type: application/xml Accept: application/json Cache-Control: no-cache
