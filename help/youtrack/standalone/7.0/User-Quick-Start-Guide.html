New Users - Quick Start Guide

If you are new to YouTrack, we recommend that you take a look at the following topics.

TopicDescription
Create and Edit IssuesLearn the basic steps for creating and updating issues.
Attach FilesFlesh out issues by attaching images and files.
Watch IssuesTrack the progress of particular issues by adding them to your watch list.
Tag IssuesCategorize and track issues more effectively with tags.
SearchLocate the issues that you want to find quickly and efficiently.
Saved SearchSave a search query and share it with your team so you can all work from the same page.
CommandsFamiliarize yourself with commands that let you update one or more issues at once.
NotificationsManage how and where you receive notifications for which kind of updates.

In addition to the topics in the documentation, we have a few external resources that you might find useful.

ResourceDescription
YouTrack DemosWatch our series of short demo videos — the quickest way to get familiar with YouTrack.
YouTrack FAQRead answers to common questions that were asked by other users — maybe your questions have been answered already.
