Use Cases for Agile Boards

You can use an agile board to manage any process you can dream up. But building a board that's just right for you and your team may require a little imagination.

Here, we've collected a few board configurations that are tailored to specific use cases. Many of these examples are based on agile boards that are used by different teams here at JetBrains. Use these examples to find inspiration and customize your own boards.
