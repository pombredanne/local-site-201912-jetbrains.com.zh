Progress Indicators

The progress indicator is displayed next to the sprint selector. It shows the cards distribution by columns that are represented on the board. Move the pointer over a bar of the indicator to see the exact number of cards in the respective column.

If the number of cards in a column is less then configured Min WIP or more then Max WIP settings of the column, then the respective bar in the indicator is highlighted with the red color, just like the column itself.
