Visual Dependency Analysis

With ReSharper, you can build and analyze dependency graphs of types and projects in your solution. All visual graphs are built without compiling anything and conveniently laid out by default. Right on the graphs, you can invoke a lot of other ReSharper features including navigation, refactorings, and more.
