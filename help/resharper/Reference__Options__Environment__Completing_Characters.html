Completing Characters

ReSharper | Options | Environment | IntelliSense | Completion Characters

On this page of ReSharper options, you can set whether you want ReSharper to commit the selected completion item on pressing Space or any non-alphanumeric character. Also, you can define how the Enter and the Tab keys behave on the item selected in the completion list: the two selectors, Enter Key and Tab Key let you choose one of the following behavior options:

  • Inserts - inserts the item without replacing the text after the caret.

  • Replaces - replaces the text after the caret with the item.

Item

Description

Complete on space

Deselect this checkbox for a particular language to prevent ReSharper from committing the selected completion item on pressing Space.

Do not complete on

Type any non-alphanumeric characters without spaces for a particular language, for example .;:( to prevent ReSharper from committing the selected completion item on pressing these characters.

ReSharper's settings are saved in settings layers, which enable you to have different settings for different solutions and to share your settings with your team. To learn more, see Manage and Share ReSharper Settings.
