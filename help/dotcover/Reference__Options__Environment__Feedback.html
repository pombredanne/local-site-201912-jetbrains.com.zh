Product Feedback

ReSharper | Options | Environment | Product Feedback

A while after you have installed dotCover, it might invoke a balloon in the system tray to invite you to join the dotCover Feedback Program in order to help dotCover developers improve the product based on anonymous usage statistics.

When you click the balloon, the Product Feedback prompt appears, letting you make a decision whether to participate or not.

This page serves two purposes:

  • It sets forward comprehensive conditions of participation in the program.

  • It provides the Participate anonymously in Feedback program check box to either join the program or resign at any time.

dotCover's settings are saved in settings layers, which enable you to have different settings for different solutions and to share your settings with your team. To learn more, see Managing and Sharing dotCover Settings.
