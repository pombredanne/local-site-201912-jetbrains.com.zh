Auto-Deleting Workspaces

You can automate the cleanup of your workspace files using the auto-deletion feature. dotMemory will automatically delete workspace files that were not opened* a certain amount of days. If you want to prevent a workspace from auto-deletion, you can pin it in the list of workspaces.

* This includes only workspaces opened in dotMemory. Workspace files accessed in other ways (copied, opened in external editors, pinned and then unpinned in dotMemory, etc.) are ignored.

To enable workspace auto-deletion

  1. On Home page, choose the Preferences tab.

  2. In the Save Workspaces section, in Auto-delete workspaces that were not opened in last [ ] days, specify how many days workspaces must not be opened to qualify for auto-deletion.

  3. Click Save Changes.

To prevent a particular workspace from auto-deletion

  1. On the Home page, on the left panel, choose Recent | Workspaces.

  2. In the list of workspaces, click the pin button against the workspace you want to prevent from auto-deletion. You can "unpin" the workspace back at any time.
