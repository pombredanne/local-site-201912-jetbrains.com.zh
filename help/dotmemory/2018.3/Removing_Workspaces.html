Deleting Workspaces

To delete a particular workspace

  1. On the Home page, on the left panel, choose Recent | Workspaces.

  2. In the list of workspaces, find a workspace you want to delete and click the corresponding delete button.

To delete all workspaces

  1. On the Home page, on the left panel, choose Recent | Workspaces.

  2. Click the Delete All link located above the workspaces.

You can also force dotMemory to delete all workspaces that were not prevented from auto-deletion (pinned).

To delete all unpinned workspaces

  1. On the Home page, on the left panel, choose Recent | Workspaces.

  2. Click the Delete Unpinned link located above the workspaces.
