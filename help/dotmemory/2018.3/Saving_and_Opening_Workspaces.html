Saving and Opening Workspaces

By default, once the profiling is finished, workspace files are saved automatically to C:\Users\[username]\AppData\Local\JetBrains\dotMemory\[version]\Workspaces.

To change the default workspace files location

  1. On the Home page, open Preferences.

  2. In the Save Workspaces section, specify a new location in the Workspace files location field.

To open a workspace

  1. On the Home page, on the left panel, choose Recent | Workspaces.

  2. In the list of workspaces, find a desired workspace and click the corresponding Open button.
