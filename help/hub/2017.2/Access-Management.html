Access Management

Access management is one of the primary features of Hub. Use Hub to let users log in and access the applications and services that are connected to the Hub platform. The options described in this section are available from the Access Management section of the Administration menu.

OptionDescription
UsersManage user accounts and their access rights in connected services.
GroupsAssign users to collections that let you assign roles, permissions, and licences to multiple users at once.
RolesDefine a collection of permissions that you can assign to single users and groups.
Auth ModulesConnect Hub to third-party authentication services.
SAML 2.0Use Hub as an Identify Provider for other web-based applications.
