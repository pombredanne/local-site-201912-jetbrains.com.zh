Services

A service is any application or external service that can be connected and registered in Hub.

Hub includes two services that are pre-installed — a Project Wizard and a Dashboard. These services extend the functionality of the application, but do not provide any resources to Hub.

Hub 1.0 supports connections to the JetBrains team collaboration tools YouTrack and Upsource.
