Register a Service Provider

When you configured Hub as the SAML IdP for your service, the final step to enable the authorization with SAML is to register your service as the service provider in Hub.

Register your service as the Service Provider in Hub

  1. Open the More settings > SAML 2.0 > Registered Service Providers tab.
  2. Click the Register Service Provider... button.
  3. Enter parameters of the service as the SAML service provider.

Parameters of a Service Provider

You need to specify the following parameters of your service:

ParameterDescription
NameThe name of the Service Provider to be displayed in Hub.
IssuerThe entity_id of your service as the SAML Service Provider.
DescriptionOptional description of the service.
Consumer URLThe Assertion Consumer Service (ACS) URL of the service. When Hub is set as the IdP, it posts the SAML login response to this URL.
Hub should send LogoutResponseThis option defines if Hub should send LogoutResponse to a service provider. By default, the options is disabled. For the sample configurations in our documentation, we specify the required state of the option for each particular service provider.
