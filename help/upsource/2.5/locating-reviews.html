Locating reviews

There is a number of ways to discover reviews and their status across the entire project.

To see the reviews you've created or have been added to as a participant, you can:

  1. Track recently added or changed reviews in the default My news view of the news feed, located on the right pane of the project home page.
  2. Go to the default My reviews view of the Reviews tab:

The reviews are divided into the following groups:

  • Ready to close - reviews which have been completed by reviewers and which you are expected to close
  • To review - reviews in which you are expected to participate as a reviewer
  • Authored by you - reviews you created

You can browse all reviews in the project by scrolling through the revision list:

Revisions under a review are marked with a clickable Review icon linking to the review home page. Closed reviews are greyed out.

You can also search for specific reviews using various search parameters.
