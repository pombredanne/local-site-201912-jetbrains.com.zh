Searching for branches

To find a specific branch:

  1. Go to the Branches page by clicking the link on top of the revisions list:

  2. Enter your query in the search text field or use a drop-down menu to select predefined queries and keywords:

    Your recent queries can be also found there.

    Branches can be searched by:

    • name (i.e. name: {patch-2.3}

    • or predefined keywords:

      • #is-pull-request — branches that are pull-requests

      • #commits-ahead — branches that are ahead of their parent branch

  3. You can also sort the results in a preferred order:
