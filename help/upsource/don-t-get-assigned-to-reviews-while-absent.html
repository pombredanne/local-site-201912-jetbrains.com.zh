Don't get assigned to reviews while absent

Upsource can assign you to a review as a reviewer or watcher under a custom workflow. If you're planning to be on leave, you can enable the Out of office mode for a specified number of days. This will pause automated review assignments and email reminders to review changes in existing reviews while you are absent. Other notifications will still be sent.
