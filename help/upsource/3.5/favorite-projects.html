Favorite projects

For a quick access to a project that you open often you can add it to Favorites - just open that project and click the star icon next to its name:

The project will be now pinned at the top of the entry page. To remove it from Favorites, click the star again.

Reviews and news feed events displayed on the entry page are derived from "starred" projects only.
