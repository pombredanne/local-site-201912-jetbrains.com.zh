Searching for text

Upsource lets you search the source code for text occurrences across all projects.

To perform a full-text search:

  1. Click the search icon in the top-right corner and enter your text query into the omni-search box:

  2. Click Run full-text search.. or just hit Enter.

    You'll be redirected to a text search page where your search results will be listed.

  3. Narrow down your search to a particular project selecting it from the drop-down list:

  4. For more search options, click Advanced search:

    You can:

    • limit your search to specific file types

    • exclude deleted files from search
