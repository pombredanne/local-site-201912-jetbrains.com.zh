Enabling LDAP and third-party account authentication

LDAP authentication module allows users to log in to various JetBrains services connected to Hub with credentials stored in your directory service. To configure and enable LDAP, follow these instructions.

The other authentication modules make it possible to log in with third-party account credentials.

  • Logging in with GitHub account
  • Logging in with Google
  • Logging in with Atlassian Jira
  • Logging in with AOL
  • Logging in with OpenID
  • Logging in with Yahoo
