Choosing a Method to Step Into

When you reach a line with calls of several methods, you can choose the method you want to step into.

If you choose a method of a class stepping into which is suppressed on the Debugger. Stepping page of the Settings dialog, the suppression is overridden as when you invoke the Force Step Into command.

  1. Click on the stepping toolbar, or press Shift+F7, or select Run | Smart Step Into from the main menu.

  2. In the popup, select the desired method from the list.
