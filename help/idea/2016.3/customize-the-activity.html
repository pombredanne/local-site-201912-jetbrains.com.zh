Customize the Activity

IntelliJ IDEA lets you customize the activity you have selected in the Add an Activity dialog.

Activity NameUse this field to specify the name of your activity.
Layout NameUse this field to specify the layout name.
TitleUse this field to specify the name of the title.

The appearance of other fields, drop-down lists and check boxes depend on the type of form factors and activities you have previously selected.
