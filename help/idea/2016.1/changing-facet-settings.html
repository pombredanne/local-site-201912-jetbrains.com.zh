Changing Facet Settings

Facet settings vary depending on the facet types. This section provides only general instructions for changing these settings. Facet-specific options are discussed elsewhere.

To change facet settings

  1. Open the Project Structure dialog (Ctrl+Shift+Alt+S).
  2. In the left-hand pane, click Modules.
  3. Expand the necessary module node to see the facets associated with this module.
  4. Click the desired facet. The facet settings are shown in the right-hand part of the dialog.
  5. Modify the facet settings as required and click OK.
