Enabling Support of Additional Live Templates

Support of additional templates includes more than 200 different HTML, CSS, and XSL live templates. All of them are listed under the Emmet nodes on the Live Templates page of the Settings dialog box.

To enable support of additional HTML, CSS, and XSL live templates

  1. Open the Settings / Preferences Dialog by choosing File | Settings for Windows and Linux or IntelliJ IDEA | Preferences for OS X, and click Live Templates under Editor.
  2. On the Live Templates page, that opens, select the check boxes next to the relevant template groups.
