Textual Search

With JetBrains Rider, you can search and replace text in the same way as in most editors and IDEs:

  • To search or replace text in the current document, press Ctrl+F/Ctrl+H or choose Edit | Find | Find/Replace in the menu.

  • To search or replace in the desired scope, press Ctrl+Shift+F/Ctrl+Shift+H or choose Edit | Find | Find/Replace in Path in the menu.

Another option of searching text in the current document is pressing Ctrl+F3 on any word to find all occurrences of this word.
