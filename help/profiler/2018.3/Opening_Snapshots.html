Opening Snapshots

To open a snapshot taken on the current computer

  • In the dotTrace Home window, do one of the following:

      1. On the left panel, choose Snapshots.

      2. In the list of snapshots, find a desired snapshot and click the corresponding Open button.

      1. On the left panel, choose Launches.

      2. Find a profiling launch configuration which was used to collect a desired snapshot.

      3. Expand the profiling launch configuration by clicking >.

      4. In the list of snapshots, find a desired snapshot and click the corresponding Open button.

To open a .dtt or .dtp snapshot file (e.g., taken on another computer)

  1. In the dotTrace Home window, on the left panel, click Open Snapshots.

  2. In the opened window, select the desired file and click Open.

The file will be opened in the corresponding viewer.
