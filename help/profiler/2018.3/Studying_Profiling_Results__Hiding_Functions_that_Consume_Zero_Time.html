Hiding Functions that Consume Zero Time

dotTrace enables you to specify whether or not to display function calls that consume 0% of root time. To hide such functions, choose View | Show Calls Consuming 0% on the menu bar or click Show Calls Consuming 0% on the toolbar. It also helps clarify the tree and makes it easier to analyze.
