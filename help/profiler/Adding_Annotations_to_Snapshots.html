Adding Annotations to Snapshots

Annotations allow you to better distinguish snapshots between each other. Annotations are shown below snapshots in the Recent | Snapshots and Recent | Launches lists.

To add an annotation to a snapshot

  1. In the dotTrace Home window, do one of the following:

      1. On the left panel, choose Recent | Snapshots.

      2. In the list of snapshots, find a desired snapshot and click the corresponding button.

      1. On the left panel, choose Recent | Launches.

      2. Find a profiling launch configuration which was used to collect a desired snapshot.

      3. Expand the profiling launch configuration by clicking >.

      4. In the list of snapshots, find a desired snapshot and click the corresponding button.

  2. Enter annotation text and click Save.
