Navigation Between Bookmarks

FunctionShortcutUse this shortcut to...
Go to Bookmark <number> Ctrl+Number Navigate to a numbered bookmark with the corresponding number.
Toggle Bookmark F11 Turn anonymous bookmark on or off.
Toggle Bookmark with Mnemonic Ctrl+F11 Turn bookmark with mnemonic on or off.
Show Bookmarks Shift+F11 Open Bookmarks dialog to manage existing bookmarks and navigate between them.
