Using Online Resources

If the online WebStorm documentation fails to answer your questions, you can find more information on the web. The following resources are available:

  • Official JetBrains home page
  • PhpStorm and WebStorm community
  • WebStorm Blog
  • WebStorm Twitter
  • WebStorm Plugin Repository

Finally, do not miss the JetBrains TV (Help | JetBrains TV). On the JetBrains TV, choose the WebStorm channel at WebStorm Videos, and watch screencasts.
