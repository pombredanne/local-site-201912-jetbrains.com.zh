Navigating to a Concept Declaration or Editor Declaration

To navigate to a concept declaration or an editor declaration, follow these steps

  1. Open a node in the editor.

  2. Place the caret at the desired node.

  3. On the main menu, point to Navigate, and then select Concept Declaration or Editor Declaration respectively.
